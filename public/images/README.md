# Drop generated images here

Use the exact filenames from `IMAGE_BRIEF.md` (repo root), e.g.:

- `opportunity-system.jpg`
- `tech-apply.jpg`
- `opportunity-reach.jpg`
- `opportunity-manufacturing.jpg`
- `company-mission.jpg`
- `tech-biomimicry-photo.jpg` (optional)

Long edge ≥ 2000px, JPG or WebP, no text/logos in the image. Once they are here,
they get wired into `lib/images.ts` and the matching `<ImageSlot>`s.
