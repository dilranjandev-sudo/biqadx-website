# BIQADX — Image Generation Manifest

Generate each image below, save it with the **exact filename** into `public/images/`,
and hand it back. I then register it in `lib/images.ts` (one line) and it replaces the
on-page placeholder automatically.

## Non-negotiable compliance (every image)
- **No text, words, numbers, logos, or UI** rendered in the image.
- **No device screen showing a result** — screens are off/dark/blank. No "positive/negative",
  no checkmarks, no readouts. (This is what disqualified earlier images.)
- **No fabricated clinical data or evidence.** People are generic and respectful — never a
  portrait of a real founder/team member.
- No emoji/pictographs.

## House style (shared, keep consistent)
Photorealistic editorial/product or documentary photography — **real, not AI-looking**.
Deep near-black (#0B0E14) backgrounds where dark; one thin directional band of cyan→violet→amber
iridescence on engineered surfaces only (never a flat rainbow). Soft key light, shallow depth of
field, fine film grain, low-key cinematic grade. Match the home-page set.
